MECHANICS

MECHANICS

The CAD team designs the robot's structure and components using advanced 3D modeling software. They create detailed blueprints that guide the mechanics and electronics teams, ensuring all parts are optimized for performance in competitions.

ELECTRONICS

The Electronics team integrates sensors, motors, and control systems to ensure our robot operates efficiently. Their collaboration with other teams is essential for optimizing the robot's performance in competitions, guaranteeing reliability and success.

PROGRAMING

The Programming team develops software to control the robot's functions, from autonomous operations to manual control. They collaborate closely with the Electronics team, continually refining the robot's performance through rigorous testing and adjustments.

Meet with the Magnetar Vision

OUR VISION

As the Magnetar FRC team, our vision is to be recognized as a leading team in both national and international robotics arenas, inspiring the engineers of the future by reaching broader platforms. We aim to achieve global sustainable success by nurturing individuals in the STEM field who possess 21st-century skills, following technological innovations, and effectively utilizing them to develop projects. One of our primary goals is to ensure the sustainability of the team by leading our students, who are trained with the Magnetar discipline, to pass on their experiences to the new generations.

OUR MISSION

As the Magnetar FRC team, our mission is to make STEM accessible to everyone without discrimination by increasing interest in technology and engineering at a young age. We aim to use the FRC platform to develop analytical thinking, problem-solving, and collaboration skills. Our goal is to achieve the highest level of success in every competition while also contributing to our community and future by raising awareness of the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) within the scope of 21st-century skills.
