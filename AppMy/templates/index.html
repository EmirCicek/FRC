Our Team

Magnetar is a robotics team entirely run by students. Our team consists of 44 young people who are passionate about technology and engineering, and mentors who guide us. We have achieved significant success on various national and international platforms such as robotics competitions and olympiads. As Magnetar, we act with a team spirit that fosters innovation, promotes STEM, and enhances problem-solving skills.
